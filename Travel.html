Local Destination


Maasai Mara

Situated in south-west Kenya and is one of Africa's Greatest Wildlife Reserves.


Diani

The sparkling white sands and lush greenery of Diani invite you to relax under a beach umbrella with a refreshing drink.


Amboseli National Reserve

One of the best places in Africa to view large herds of elephants up close.


Tsavo National Park

Tsavo East is famous for photo-worthy sightings of large elephant herds rolling and bathing in red dust.
