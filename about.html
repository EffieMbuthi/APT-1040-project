Executive Founder
Brief biography

“Travelling - it leaves you speechless, then turns you into a storyteller.” Ibn Battuta. Living without some sort of excitement, as I see it, is a jail term. Every day meshes into the next till they are all the same. Get up. Leave for work. Consume your dinner. Binge watch movies and series . Take a nap. Time flies, in its nature, and we are left feeling unaccomplished. Truth is, we are all here for a larger cause that we should embrace. Travel brings me excitement. It is my passion. My goal. I convey one such desire using credible experiences I have had, to inspire others to be bold enough to embark on the adventures of their wildest fantasies. It is for this reason that I founded Cyliar Stretch, a travelling agency built to succor Kenyan people and tourist, in whole, discover parts of Kenya and of the world.
